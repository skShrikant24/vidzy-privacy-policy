Privacy Policy

VidZ operates the VidZ mobile application as an e-learning and student engagement platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users of the Service.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Address
  • Gender or sex, if provided by the user
  • Profile photo, if uploaded by the user
  • Course enrollment, learning progress, and course activity
  • Attendance-related information submitted through barcode or QR scanning features
  • Account and session information required to keep you signed in and provide app functionality

How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Create, manage, and maintain user accounts
  • Authenticate users by phone number, OTP, or password
  • Provide access to courses, learning content, and student features
  • Display and update user profile information
  • Track course participation, learning progress, and related activity
  • Support attendance features, including barcode or QR-based attendance workflows
  • Provide customer support and important service-related communication
  • Maintain app security and basic operational functionality

Camera and Photo Library Access

The app may request access to the device camera and photo library only when needed for app features selected by the user.

  • Camera access may be used to capture a profile photo
  • Camera access may also be used to scan barcodes or QR codes for attendance-related features
  • Photo library access may be used to select a profile photo from the user’s device

These permissions are used only to provide the relevant app functionality and are not used for cross-app tracking.

Local Device Storage

The app may store limited information locally on your device, such as login session data, mobile number, user profile details, and profile photo path, in order to keep you signed in and improve the user experience.

Tracking

VidZ does not track users across third-party apps, websites, or services for advertising purposes. VidZ does not use personal information collected in the app for third-party targeted advertising or data brokerage.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party software libraries or service providers that are necessary for app functionality, such as content display, sharing, media playback, login support, or other technical operations. These services may process limited information only as needed to support the app’s features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell users’ personal information. We may share information only as needed to operate the Service, comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or support core functionality provided through our service providers or backend systems.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

VidZ does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without appropriate authorization where required. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us inappropriately, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

User Choices and Account Information

Users may review and update certain profile information within the app. Where account deletion is supported, users may request deletion of their account and associated data through the method provided in the app or through our support process.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a revised effective date. Continued use of the Service after changes become effective means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
